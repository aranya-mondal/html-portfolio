The best movies according to Aranya

My top 3 movies of all time

3 Idiots

This movie is a must watch. It shows the reality of the challenges faced by the Indian students due to the flaws of the Indian education system.

Taare Zameen Par

This movie shows that everybody is unique and we should not judge a fish by its ability to climb a tree.

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara

This movie gives us a reality check of what are the most essential things in life and also in this movie we can get a beautiful view of Spain.
